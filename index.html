About Naomi

Naomi is a visionary makeup artist currently pursuing her cosmetology license. With experience on set for independent films and a love for event makeup and styling in particular, she brings creativity and attention to detail to every project. With a lifelong passion for beauty, Naomi is excited to grow her career and looks forward to connecting with new clients and collaborators.
